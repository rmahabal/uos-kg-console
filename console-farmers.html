Can a 17-year-old open a Student Checking account, and what does it cost?
⚠ Generic LLM · no graphconfident, ungrounded
No source. The model is pattern-matching from training data — plausible, not this institution's truth, and impossible to audit.
✓ Grounded on FIFCU graphcited · safe when unsure
The grounded agent does three things a bare model can't: it answers in FIFCU's actual products and rules with citations, it defers where the institution hasn't confirmed a value (NEEDS_FI) instead of inventing one, and it declines where the needed knowledge isn't loaded — safe uncertainty over confident error.
